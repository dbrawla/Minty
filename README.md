A HackDavis 2025 project designed to make saving money fun, social, and motivating.

Minty turns personal budgeting into a game by allowing users to grow a virtual plant as they meet their savings goals.
Designed and built by a 3-person team during the 24-hour HackDavis hackathon, Minty combines behavioral psychology, UI design, and smart data handling to help students stay financially aware.
